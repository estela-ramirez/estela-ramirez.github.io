Gradient Snake

Snake Game with a colorful Snake and a bad apple

A throwback game. The rules are simple, there is a snake (gradient colored), a good apple (gradient colored), and a bad apple (red). The goal is to have the snake eat the good apple to gain a higher score. One good apple equates one point. If the snake eats the bad apple, the score is reduced by five points. If the score goes below zero, the game ends. The game also ends if the snake goes out of bounds.
