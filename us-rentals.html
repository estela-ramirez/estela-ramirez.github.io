US Rentals

A web app that visualizes rental prices across the US

This web app was intended to help me visualize where in the country is a good city to move to after college. Rent prices play an important role in that decision and thus I decided to look at rent prices across the U.S. The dataset itself has average rental prices across the top 100 most populated cities + 5 smaller cities. Therefore, not all states are represented in the chloropleth graph.

There are 3 different graphs and a table to look at the data

  • Table: filter data by ID, region (city), state, population rank
  • States Map: chloropleth map of 2021 average rental prices across state
  • Bar Graph: 2 bar graphs for top 10 most expensive & least expensive cities in 2021
  • Line Graph: compare up to 3 cities by 2021 average rent price
